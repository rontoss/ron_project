How to play rock, paper, scissors:

  • Rock beats scissors ("rock crushes scissors" or sometimes "blunts scissors");
  • Paper beats rock ("paper covers rock");
  • Scissors beats paper ("scissors cuts paper");
  • If the same shape is chosen, the game is tied and is usually immediately replayed to break the tie.
